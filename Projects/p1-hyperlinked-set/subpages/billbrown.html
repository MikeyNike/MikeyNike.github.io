Bill Brown works with an emphasis on "object relations" asking questions of how inanimate objects enable human subjects (individually and collectively) to form and transform themselves and indeed how objects and subjects transform one another. In a short piece called "Thing Theory," he points out how things and thingness might become new objects of critical analysis.
